About FloraDaily

At FloraDaily, we believe every flower tells a story. From joyful celebrations to heartfelt moments, our handcrafted floral arrangements and carefully curated gift bundles are designed to create lasting memories. With a passion for quality, creativity, and timely delivery, we make every occasion more beautiful and meaningful.

Our Story

FloraDaily was founded in 2022 with a vision of making flower gifting more thoughtful, elegant, and accessible. What began as a small local flower boutique has grown into a trusted flower and gift delivery service, offering handcrafted bouquets and premium gift collections for every occasion. Over the past 4 years, we have continuously expanded our product range, strengthened our delivery network, and built a passionate team dedicated to delivering fresh flowers and memorable experiences. Every bouquet we create reflects our commitment to quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction.

Fresh Flower Sourcing

We carefully source our flowers from trusted local growers and premium flower farms to ensure every bouquet is crafted using the freshest blooms available. Each flower is handpicked, quality-checked, and transported under controlled conditions to preserve its freshness, fragrance, and vibrant colors. By working closely with experienced growers who follow sustainable farming practices, FloraDaily guarantees high-quality floral arrangements that bring beauty, elegance, and lasting impressions to every celebration.
